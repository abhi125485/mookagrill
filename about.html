About Mooka Grill

Where Thai tradition meets modern taste.

Our Story

Founded in 2020, Mooka Grill brings the authentic Thai Mookata dining experience to food lovers around the world. Combining the joy of barbecue and hotpot, we’ve built a place where families and friends gather for good food and warm memories.

Our chefs craft every dish using traditional Thai recipes and the freshest local ingredients — balancing bold spices and delicate flavors for a truly memorable meal.

Our Philosophy

We believe that food is more than taste — it’s connection, culture, and comfort. At Mooka Grill, every ingredient and every smile is part of our mission to share the warmth of Thai hospitality.
